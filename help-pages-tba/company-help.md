## Getting Started

### How do I get started with a Company Account?

1.  Navigate to [zamhack.com](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://zamhack.com) and click **Signup**.
2.  **Crucial Step:** Ensure you select the **Company** registration toggle (not Student).
3.  Fill in your organization details and log in to be redirected to your **Company Portal**.

### Where can I see a summary of my account activity?

The **Dashboard** provides a "Snapshot" at the top of the page. You can instantly see:

  * **Active Challenges:** Projects currently live.
  * **Participants:** Number of students engaged.
  * **Submissions:** Pending feedback requests.

### How do I review a student's submission?

1.  On the Dashboard, scroll to the **Recent Submissions** table.
2.  Locate the student’s name and the specific challenge.
3.  Click the orange **Review** button to view their work, grade it, or leave comments.

## Managing Challenges

### How do I launch a new Challenge?

You can start a project via:

  * **Sidebar:** Click **Challenges** in the left-hand navigation menu.
  * **Quick Actions:** Click **Create Challenge** on the right side of the Dashboard.

### The 5-Step Creation Wizard

1.  **Basic Info:** Set title, description, industry, and difficulty. Choose "Solo" or "Team" and set participant limits.
2.  **Timeline:** Pick start/end dates and optional registration deadlines.
3.  **Milestones:** Break the project into phases. Require a **GitHub Link**, **Demo URL**, or **Written Report**.
4.  **Skills:** Tag technical skills (e.g., Python, UI Design). *Note: Press **Enter** after typing each skill to save the tag.*
5.  **Review:** Double-check details and save as a draft.

### How do I make a challenge live?

Once finished with the wizard:

1.  Go to the **Challenge Overview**.
2.  Click the green **Submit for Approval** button (top right).
3.  Once the ZamHack admin team verifies it, your challenge goes live\!

## Talent Search & Messaging

### How do I find specific students?

Navigate to the **Talent Search** tab in the left sidebar. You can find talent using:

  * **Search Bar:** Search by name, school, or degree.
  * **Sorting:** Sort by Newest, Name, Experience, or Activity.
  * **Experience Filters:** Filter by "Has Completed Challenges" or "Currently Active."

### How do I start a conversation?

  * **From Talent Search:** Click the orange **Message** button on a student’s card.
  * **From Sidebar:** Click the **Messages** tab to view your inbox. Unread messages are marked with a red notification badge.

## Organization Settings

### Managing your Public Profile

Click **Org Settings** in the left sidebar to update:

  * **Company Identity:** Display name and industry.
  * **Online Presence:** Website URL and Company Logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG).
  * **About:** A compelling description of your mission.
  * **Note:** Always click **Save Changes** at the bottom right.

-----

## Account Settings & Support

### Personal Profile & Security

Click **Settings** at the bottom of the sidebar to manage:

  * **Personal Bio:** Helps students know who they are communicating with.
  * **Password:** Update via the **Account Security** section (min. 6 characters).
  * **Danger Zone:** The **Delete Account** option is permanent and cannot be undone.

### How do I get help?

Click **Admin Support** in the sidebar:

  * **Quick Topics:** Use pre-filled subjects like "Challenge Setup Help."
  * **Response Time:** Mon–Fri, 8 AM – 5 PM PHT (usually within 24 hours).
  * **Direct Email:** [support@zamhack.com](mailto:support@zamhack.com).

> [\!TIP]
> Always **Log out** via the button at the bottom of the sidebar when using a shared computer to ensure your session remains secure.
